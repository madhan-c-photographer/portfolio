About Me

Who I Am

I'm a graduate who completed my B.Com degree at Dr. N.G.P Arts and Science College, Coimbatore from 2022-2025, while developing my passion for visual storytelling and creative composition.

My academic journey has provided me with a strong foundation in business studies, complemented by my exploration of photography as a creative outlet. I specialize in portrait and landscape photography, using both natural and artificial lighting to create compelling visuals.

Beyond my formal education, I'm deeply committed to the art of photography and visual arts, constantly refining my skills through workshops and hands-on practice to create inspiring visual stories.
